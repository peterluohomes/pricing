The First Week Price Signal
Sales Price / Listing Price vs. First Week Showings
📊 Key Insight More first-week showings = higher sale price. Homes with 30+ showings close at 110–120% of asking. Under 10 showings typically close at 89–95%. Low showings in week one is not bad luck — it's a pricing signal.
